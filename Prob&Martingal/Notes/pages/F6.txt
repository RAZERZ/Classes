"[latex]Define $\sigma$-finite measure[/latex]";"[latex]We say a measure $\mu$ is $\sigma$-finite if we can split it into finite measures
\begin{equation*}
\begin{gathered}
\exists A_n\in S\text{ s.t } S = \bigcup_{n=1}^{\infty}A_n, \mu(A_n)<\infty\quad\forall n\in \N
\end{gathered}
\end{equation*}\par
\noindent Example is a Lebesgue measure[/latex]"
